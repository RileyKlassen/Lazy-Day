The Crowd Is Mesmerized.

You're tired of holding back, you want to be the guy who turns heads, and that starts tonight. You push your way through the crowd and call next on the mic. It's your turn, and the song you choose is Wuthering Heights by Kate Bush. You get up on stage and and look out on the crowd. Oh God, what have I done? I can't sing! I can't do this! But the crowd begins to chant: 'Come home, Cathy. Come home, Cathy!' There's no backing down. You close your eyes, do your best Falsetto, and hope for the best... When you open your eyes, everyone's mouth is on the floor, and you can't tell if they loved it or hated it, but they're screaming and carrying you around the bar. One thing is certain, you turned some heads.
