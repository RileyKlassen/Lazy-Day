Welcome To An Interactive Storybook.

My name is Riley Klassen, and this is my first exploration of coding as a storytelling method. The way this works is simple, once you click the start button you will be presented with a story card, which includes an image and text. You will have two options as to how you would like the story to progress, each with its own path. There are a total of 8 different, surreal endings. Enjoy
