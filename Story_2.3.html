Your Cereal is Sentient.

You pour your cereal in to a bowl and add milk. About half way through your meal, you look down and see something peculiar: the letter shaped cereal pieces spell out your name. A moment later, they rearange and read a different message. This one says 'It is I, God'. The cheap snack continues, explaining that you are the child of God. Not until you showed initiative by getting out of bed early and going to the bar less did she choose to make contact. The cereal declares it is time for you to attend your heavenly duties. This whole thing sounds a little bit like a scam, but then, maybe it's true. Will you heed god's call?
